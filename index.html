TIMELINE


Born and BroughtUp

(1976-1985)


Pichai Sundararajan born June 10, 1972 better known as Sundar Pichai
in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. He grew up in a two-room apartment
At an early age Pichai displayed an interest in technology
and an extraordinary memory, especially for telephone numbers.


Education

Pichai began his career as a materials engineer
Following a short stint at the management consulting firm McKinsey & Co.
Pichai completed schooling in Jawahar Vidyalaya Senior Secondary School in Ashok Nagar, Chennai
He completed the Class XII from Vana Vani school at IIT Madras.


Working Experience

Sundar Pichai began his career as a materials engineer before briefly working at the management consulting firm McKinsey & Co.
He then shifted gears and joined Google in 2004
Pichai spearheaded innovation and product management for various client software products such as Chrome, ChromeOS, and Drive.


Accomplishments

Sundar Pichai played a key role in the development of popular apps like Gmail and Maps
led the open-sourcing of the VP8 video codec and introduced the WebM format
Chromebooks debuted in 2012, and by 2013, Pichai had taken on the additional responsibility of Android.
In 2015, Pichai succeeded Larry Page as CEO of Google, coinciding with the formation of Alphabet Inc.
